Hello, I'm Casey.

I'm a social entrepreneur.

Skills

I'm currently working on a project to increase access to happiness as well as learning to code. I recently returned to the Bay Area from Beijing, where I founded China's first person-to-person microfinance non-profit, called Wokai. Over 5 years, I led the organization from an idea to a live platform with 3,000 contributors financing around 1,000 rural poor to start their own businesses. I led a passionate core team of 7 in Beijing as well as 350 volunteers in our 20 Chapter cities in Asia and North America. Over the course of my time with Wokai as well as my personal dabblings, I've developed a strong skillset in the following areas:

Business in China

I pioneered a model that hadn't existed before in China and didn't yet have a regulatory environment to support it. In the process, I engaged in almost every level of business in China, from launching national level government partnerships and working with rural government officials, to collaborating with major nation-wide brands and media, to working in rural areas with local NGOs and micro-entrepreneurs.

Branding & PR

I love stories and the creative process of infusing a vision into the face and DNA of an organization. At Wokai, I led every stage of branding, PR, and communications from an initial concept to a globally recognized brand. I was fortunate enough to secure features in major Western and Chinese media, including CNN, Bloomberg, CBS, USA Today, CCTV, Phoenix TV, China Newsweek, and the South China Morning Post.

Business Development

Engaging with stakeholders to acheive lofty goals energizes me. I led Wokai's fundraising and business development efforts for 5+ years, raising $1.12 million to support our operations and micro-loans, funding almost 1,000 micro-entrepreneurs in China to start small business. I also established 15 corporate partnerships with firms including Cathay Pacific and Morrison & Foerster, and launched a strategic partnership with the China Population Welfare Foundation (a government foundation), making Wokai one of the first foreign non-profit organizations licensed to fundraise publically in Mainland China.

Product, Coding & Design

After 5+ years of working with designers and developers to lead product efforts from the sidelines, I made the leap to learning to design and code myself. I've spent the past 4 months stuyding frontend development (HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Jquery) and user-interface design. I'm about to dive into back-end development. In the near future, I'm hoping to be able to build out my ideas, end-to-end.
